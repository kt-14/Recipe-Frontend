Shahi Tukda

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread, 1 cup sugar, 1/2 cup water, 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1 cup milk, 1/2 cup condensed milk, 1/4 cup ghee, 1/4 cup chopped nuts

Recipe

  1. Cut bread slices into triangles and fry in ghee until golden brown.
  2. Boil water and sugar together until it forms a syrup; add cardamom powder.
  3. Boil milk and condensed milk until thickened.
  4. Dip fried bread in sugar syrup, layer on a plate, pour rabri over, and garnish with nuts.

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk (for paneer), 1 liter milk (for rabri), 1 cup sugar, 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar, 1/2 tsp cardamom powder, a few saffron strands, chopped nuts

Recipe

  1. Boil 1 liter milk, curdle with lemon juice, strain and press to form paneer, then knead until smooth.
  2. Shape paneer into small, flat discs, and boil in sugar water syrup for 10 minutes; let cool.
  3. Boil remaining milk, reduce to half, add powdered sugar, cardamom, and saffron.
  4. Soak the paneer discs in the rabri, chill, and garnish with chopped nuts before serving.
